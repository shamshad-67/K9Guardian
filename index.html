Everything you need to know

The Vanguard Harness comes in S, M, L, and XL, fitted by chest girth (15–20", 20–28", 28–36", 36–44"). Wrap a soft tape around the widest part of your dog's ribcage, just behind the front legs, and match it to our size guide. If your dog falls between sizes, we recommend sizing up for thick-coated breeds.

The front-chest D-ring redirects your dog's momentum sideways the moment they pull, which interrupts the forward charge without any pressure on the throat. Most owners report a noticeable difference within the first few walks, especially combined with consistent leash training.

Yes. The 5-point adjustment system locks around the chest and ribcage rather than just the neck, so there's no loose loop for a dog to back out of, even mid-panic. We stress-tested it specifically with known "Houdini" dogs during development.

The outer shell is 1000D Cordura nylon, the same fiber class used in military and tactical packs. The lining is an aerated neoprene-foam blend, and all hardware is zinc-alloy or welded stainless steel.

Yes — hand wash is recommended for longevity, but the harness can be machine washed on a gentle, cold cycle inside a mesh bag. Air dry only; do not tumble dry, as heat can warp the buckles.

Our smallest size (S) fits chest girths from 15", which comfortably covers most adolescent and small-breed dogs. For very young puppies still growing rapidly, we recommend waiting until they're closer to adult chest size for the best fit.
